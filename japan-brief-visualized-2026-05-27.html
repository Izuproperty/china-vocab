TSMC Upgrades Kumamoto JASM-2 to 3nm, Locking In $17B and Kyushu as a High-End Node Cluster
Taiwan's Ministry of Economic Affairs authorized JASM-2's shift from 6–12nm to leading-edge 3nm on April 1, 2026. Total investment rises to ~$17B with production targeted for 2028 at 15,000 wafers/month. The upgrade materially changes the strategic calculus for Japan-listed semiconductor names.

Macro & BOJ · Story Three
Japan April CPI Falls to Four-Year Low at 1.4%, Scrambling BOJ's Rate Path
Japan's April core CPI printed at 1.4%, well below the 1.7% consensus and down sharply from 1.8% in March — the weakest reading since early 2022. The BOJ held at 0.75% on April 27–28 but now faces a genuine trilemma heading into the June 16–17 meeting with the yen at ¥159/$.
