✅ Deflation Era Ending
The 2.8% underlying gauge confirms genuine inflationary momentum. Japan exiting its 30-year low-inflation regime is the macro story of the decade — and validates BOJ's normalization mandate.
⚠️ Subsidy Distortion
Official core CPI at 1.4% will likely rise sharply when energy and education subsidies phase out. The gauge gap (140bp) is a time bomb for headline-watching bond investors holding short duration.
🔴 Yen / Oil Bind
USD/JPY near ¥159 compresses the BOJ's room to hold without triggering FX criticism; but Iran oil shock dampens growth and complicates hiking. June 16–17 meeting is a genuine binary for yen longs.
