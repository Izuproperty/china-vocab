Jensen Huang Says Nvidia Has 'Largely Conceded' China's AI Chip Market to Huawei
Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang acknowledged in a Bloomberg interview that export controls have effectively ceded the Chinese AI training chip market to Huawei — estimating Nvidia's China AI chip revenue has fallen from ~25% of total to under 5% since 2022.
