PBOC's New Liquidity Anchor: The Overnight Rate Takes Center Stage
The People's Bank of China announced that the 7-day overnight repo rate (DR007) will replace the 1-year Loan Prime Rate (LPR) as the primary signal rate for monetary policy — a structural shift toward market-based liquidity management.
