## Monetary Policy

### Bank of Japan (日本銀行, Nihon Ginkō / BOJ)
- **Governor:** Kazuo Ueda (植田和男)
- **Issue #1 (2026-05-26):** Held policy rate at 0.75% (6–3 vote, April 27-28 meeting). Raised FY2026 core CPI forecast to 2.8% from 1.9% (Iran oil impact). Cut GDP forecast to 0.5%.
- **Issue #2 (2026-05-27):** April 2026 core CPI printed 1.4% (vs 1.7% expected, vs 1.8% March) — lowest since March 2022. Core-core fell to 1.9% from 2.4%. Q1 2026 GDP: 2.1% annualized. June 16-17 meeting is live; market increasingly expects hold. Japan Times (May 21): BOJ "behind the curve" per bond market participants.
- Long-term rate normalization path under pressure: OECD projects 2.0% by end-2027
- Next meeting: June 16-17, 2026

### Ministry of Finance (財務省, Zaimushō)
- **Issue #2:** Authorized ~¥10 trillion in FX intervention (為替介入) in late April and early May 2026 after yen weakened past ¥160/dollar. First intervention since July 2024. Yen remains near ¥159 despite operations.
- Interest rate gap vs. US: BOJ 0.75% vs. Fed 3.50–3.75% (300bps differential) drives yen carry trade pressure

## Industrial Policy

### METI (経済産業省, Keizai Sangyō-shō)
- **Issue #1:** Administering ¥1 trillion NEDO AI fund (FY2026–FY2031). Overseeing Rapidus as national project. METI Minister Akazawa called Rapidus "a national project that must succeed."
- **Issue #2:** Coordinating additional 補助金 (hojokin) package for TSMC JASM-2 3nm upgrade. Government determination: JASM (high-volume) and Rapidus (small-batch) serve different markets, limiting subsidy conflict.

### JASM — Japan Advanced Semiconductor Manufacturing
- TSMC-majority joint venture; Sony Group, Toyota, Denso as minority partners
- **JASM-1:** Kumamoto, Kyushu; 12–28nm process; production ramping 2024
- **Issue #2 (JASM-2):** Technology upgrade approved April 1, 2026 (Taiwan MOEA). Original 6–12nm plan → 3nm leading-edge. $17B total investment (from $13.9B). Equipment install + production target: 2028. Capacity: 15,000 12-inch wafers/month.

## Key Policy Frameworks

### National AI Act (2026)
- Enacted within preceding 12 months of May 2026
- Prioritizes economic growth, institutional coordination, and flexible governance over immediate enforcement
- Accompanied by Basic AI Plan, draft AI IP code (near finalization), and government generative AI platform

### NEDO ¥1 Trillion AI Fund
- Running FY2026–FY2031
- Target: high-performance domestic AI models and physical AI for factory automation
- Japan AI Alliance has applied for funding
