## AI/Tech Companies

### NEC Corporation (日本電気, Nippon Denki)
- **TSE: 6701** | ~110,000 employees globally
- Systems integrator with deep relationships across Japanese central ministries, prefectural governments, and major industrials
- **Issue #2 (2026-05-27):** Became Anthropic's first Japan-based global partner. Deploying Claude Opus 4.7 to ~30,000 NEC Group engineers globally via BluStellar Scenario platform. Creating a Center of Excellence (卓越センター) for AI-native engineering. Claude integrated into Security Operations Center (SOC) services for finance, manufacturing, and local government sectors.
- "Client Zero" philosophy: NEC deploys new technology internally before offering to clients

### Anthropic (Anthropic PBC)
- **Not Japan-listed** | US AI safety company
- **Issue #2:** Designated NEC as first Japan-based global partner. Claude Opus 4.7 and Claude Code central to partnership. Also partnering with Japan's megabanks (MUFG, SMBC, Mizuho) for Claude Mythos access per government initiative.

### Preferred Networks (プリファードネットワークス, PFN)

- **Issue #1 (2026-05-26):** Contributing engineers to Japan AI Alliance on secondment basis

### Sony AI (ソニーAI)

## Policy Bodies

### METI (経済産業省, Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry)
- Oversees semiconductor and AI industrial policy
- Administering ¥1 trillion domestic AI fund via NEDO, FY2026–FY2031
- Coordinates JASM and Rapidus subsidy programs

### NEDO (国立研究開発法人新エネルギー・産業技術総合開発機構)
- National R&D agency; executing the ¥1 trillion AI fund
- Key funder of Japan AI Alliance venture

### Digital Agency (デジタル庁)
- Overseeing government digitization; potential AI deployment customer
