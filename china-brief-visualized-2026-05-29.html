China AI Chip Ecosystem: Anke-Certified Vendors + Market Position
2025 reported shipments or revenue estimates · Sources: company reports, Morgan Stanley, Tom's Hardware, May 2026
Core Claim
Anke certification creates a formal procurement pathway for domestic AI chips, locking Nvidia (and other foreign vendors) out of state and SOE budgets. Huawei Ascend holds ~41% of China's AI chip shipments and is structurally positioned to absorb the largest share of new procurement flows.
What Remains Uncertain
SMIC running at 93%+ utilization is the binding constraint: even certified chipmakers can't grow market share if wafer fab capacity is maxed. Cambricon and Kunlunxin's absence is unexplained — voluntary abstention vs. delayed submission is unclear and matters for reading the competitive landscape.
Actionable Signal
The nine-vendor list creates a de facto watch list for China's domestic AI silicon ecosystem. Listed companies (Hygon on A-shares, Alibaba/Huawei via HK proxies) are the clearest exposure vehicles. The $67B / 76% domestic share projection by 2030 is Morgan Stanley's — useful as a ceiling estimate, not a base case.

China's K-Shape Recovery: Sector Profit Growth vs. Demand Indicators · April 2026
Year-on-year percentage change · NBS data released May 27, 2026
Core Claim
Industrial profit growth is real but heavily concentrated in AI-adjacent sectors. Computing and electronics profits more than doubled YoY in April — the aggregate 24.7% figure overstates the health of the broader economy. Retail near-flat and FAI declining are not consistent with broad-based recovery.
What Remains Uncertain
Whether the electronics profit surge is durable or front-loaded (weak base period + concentrated procurement cycles). If domestic demand stays soft through H2, the K-shape widens. PPI at 2.8% is also partly commodity-driven, which is margin-supportive but not demand-confirming.
Actionable Signal
The electronics profit doubling is directionally consistent with both today's Anke chip certification story and the broader AI infrastructure capex theme. For equity positioning, the investable signal is in the AI supply chain — not in a broad China macro recovery trade. Sector selection matters more than index-level exposure.
