⚠ Risk
Achieving mass production by 2027 puts Rapidus 2+ years behind TSMC and Samsung, who started 2nm volume in Q4 2025. Process yield at scale, not prototype performance, is the critical unknown.
~ Watch
Whether the 60+ potential customers convert to binding design starts. Tape-out commitments, not MoUs, are the leading indicator. Watch for customer announcements at Japan Semiconductor Summit.
✓ Signal
Fujifilm's active co-development role is a commercial, not symbolic, signal: its photoresist and CMP slurry business gains a domestic anchor customer. Fujifilm shares are a liquid proxy on Rapidus success.

Stagflation scenario: core CPI at 2.8% while GDP slows to 0.5% limits BOJ's room to support growth via rate cuts. Energy shock from Iran conflict could force hike even against weak domestic demand.

June 16–17 BOJ meeting. Governor Ueda has explicitly maintained data dependency. If May CPI (released June 20) exceeds 2.8% on energy pass-through, pressure for a summer hike intensifies materially.
