Beijing Walls Off Its AI Frontrunners from US Capital
The NDRC instructed China's leading AI companies to reject US limited partner capital in future funding rounds — effectively bifurcating the global AI investment ecosystem and accelerating the domestication of Chinese AI financing.

Manus precedent
"Substance over form"
NDRC applied the Manus framework: even indirect US LP participation (via fund-of-funds or secondary stakes) is treated as prohibited foreign capital
